Ping Pong! Take a trip down memory lane where we play an oldie but a goodie! arcade game. The main object is to defeat your opponent by being the first one to gain points, this can only happen if the opponent misses the ball!

How to Play

By using the assigned function (up and down arrows) allows the player to move the paddle up and down to avoid the ball from pasting their paddle. The first person to reach 5 points wins, leading the game to end, GAME OVER. Enjoy the game.

Thought I'd change it up a bit, make it less boring, so be aware of the speed, as it will get faster everytime the ball hits the paddle.
